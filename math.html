Mathethesto

An educational and interactive website designed to calculate angles, areas, and perimeters of geometric shapes such as squares, triangles, rectangles, and circles with accuracy and ease. The platform allows users to input values and instantly obtain clear, reliable results, making it ideal for students, teachers, and anyone interested in mathematics.
